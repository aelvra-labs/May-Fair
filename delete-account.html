Account Deletion Request

We respect your right to control your personal data. Follow the steps below to request deletion of your account.

Step-by-Step

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to kartik@aelvra.com from your registered email address.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request — Mayfair App"
  3. Include your registered name and phone number in the email body so we can verify your identity.
  4. Our team will process your request within 7 business days and send a confirmation once completed.
Send request to
kartik@aelvra.com
Data Handling

What Data is Deleted or Retained

Upon account deletion, the following data will be handled as described:

Account credentials Deleted immediately
Profile information
(name, email, phone, address)		 Deleted within 30 days
Order history Retained up to 90 days for legal & accounting purposes
Payment records Retained up to 90 days as required by financial regulations
FCM / device tokens Deleted immediately
Wishlist & preferences Deleted within 30 days
Partial Deletion

Request Deletion of Specific Data

You may also request deletion of specific data without deleting your entire account. For example, you can request removal of your saved addresses, preferences, or order history.

To do so, mention the specific data type you wish to remove in your email request and we will process it accordingly.
